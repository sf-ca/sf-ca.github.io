Knowing Truth

Identifying Heresy—Eastern Lightning (Church of Almighty God)

1 False Christ and False Prophets Will Arise in the Last Days

  1. Prophecy of Lord Jesus (Mt 24:3–5, 10–12)
  2. Words from Apostles
    1. Paul (1Ti 4:1–2; 2Ti 4:3–4)
    2. Peter (2Pe 2:1)
    3. Jude (Jud 3, 18–19)
    4. John (1Jn 2:18; 4:1–3)

2 False Teachings of Eastern Lightning

  1. Denying the position and the work of Christ Jesus (1Jn 4:2–3; 2Jn 7–9; 2Pe 2:1)
    1. There’s another Christ—Female Christ, God incarnated again
    2. Christ’s work is incomplete—the Dispensation of Grace is expired, Female Christ has inaugurated the Dispensation of the Kingdom, Judgement of God
  2. Denying Trinity—Female Christ is the Almighty God (“No one who denies the Son has the Father.” 1Jn 2:22–23; 4:1–6)
  3. Claiming the Bible is outdated and publications by Female Christ are canonical. (Re 22:18–19; “which the ignorant and unstable twist to their own destruction, as they do the other Scriptures.” 2Pe 3:16; “does not agree with the sound words of our Lord Jesus Christ” 1Ti 6:3)
  4. Speaking lies, seared conscience (1Ti 1:4, 19; 4:1–2; Jn 8:44)

3 Impact of Heresy

  1. Cause disputes and make people lost their faith (1Ti 1:3–4)
  2. Their message will spread like cancer. Their false teaching is like a little leaven which leavens the whole lump (2Ti 2:16–18; Ga 5:7–10)
  3. Divisive (“divisive” in Tt 3:10 is αἱρετικός in Greek, transliterated as hairetikos, and in this context means heretical; Jud 17–19)

4 What Should Saints Do

  1. How to deal with heretic (Ga 1:7–9)
    1. Church leaders have to contend earnestly for the faith, charge them to teach no other doctrine, expose them, deliver them to Satan and reject them (Jud 3; 1Ti 1:3, 20; 2Tim 2:14–18; 4:14; 1Jn 2:18–19; Tt 3:10–11; 1Co 5:5)
    2. Everyone has to beware and turn away from them, do not receive them into your house nor greet them, treat them as outsider. (Mt 7:15–16; 2Ti 3:5–7; 2Jn 9–10)
  2. Principal practices for saints
    1. Pursue, hold fast to Christ Jesus and Holy Scriptures (Col 2:2–10; 2Ti 3:14–17)
    2. live godly in present age and do not stumble (1Ti 1:18–19; 2Pe 1:3–11; Jud 18–21)
    3. Preach the gospel of the kingdom (Mt 24:12–14; 2Ti 4:1–5)
